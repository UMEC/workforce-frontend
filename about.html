About

In 2016, the Utah Area Health Education Centers Program (Utah AHEC) and Utah Medical Education Council (UMEC) began developing a model to project primary care workforce requirements. Traditionally, workforce projections have considered provider-to-population ratios regardless of disease burden, efficiency of care delivery, and population demographics.

The new model is based on population health needs and transformed healthcare with team-based care. In 2018, IBM Health Corps partnered with Utah AHEC and UMEC to create a computer application with an interactive user interface to support decision making by health systems, educational programs, and policymakers.
